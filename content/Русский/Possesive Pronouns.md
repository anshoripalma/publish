## Whose?

>[!info] Note
>Whose in this case was related to the gender of the <u>**Object**</u> and has nothing to do with the **Subject**

Ex: чья книга это? *whose car (is) this?*

|  M  |  F  |  N  | Pl  |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| чей | чья | чьё | чьи |
## Your/Yours
